## Product Identity

Watch Officer is a short-session maritime decision simulator.

The player is not asked to memorize rules as a quiz. The player is placed into a compact navigational situation and must read the water, marks, traffic, radio, and instruments well enough to make a safe professional decision.

## Product Formula

Buoys define safe water. Vessels define behaviour. Radio defines discipline. Instruments define awareness. The player learns to think like a watch officer.

## Player Promise

In 5-7 minutes, the player completes one focused watchkeeping drill and receives a professional evaluation of the decision.

## Tone

- Calm.
- Serious.
- Professional.
- Maritime.
- Clear.
- Focused.
- Not arcade.
- Not noisy.
- Not childish.

## Target Player

- Recreational skippers and motor-yacht operators.
- Maritime students.
- Yacht crew learning practical watchkeeping.
- People who need short, useful drills for navigation, COLREGS, VHF/VTS, and situational awareness.

## Core Loop

1. Select a short scenario.
2. Read the briefing.
3. Control heading and speed.
4. Interpret marks, safe water, traffic, and AIS cues.
5. Make one or more safe decisions.
6. Handle one concise VHF/VTS prompt when present.
7. Complete, fail, or restart from checkpoint.
8. Receive a professional evaluation report.

## Product Pillars

### 1. Spatial Decisions, Not Rule Trivia

COLREGS and buoyage must be learned through action in space, not through selecting rule numbers.

### 2. Scenario Quality Over World Size

The game should prefer many compact high-quality scenarios over a large open world.

### 3. Calm Pressure

Pressure should come from limited time, traffic geometry, water constraints, visibility, and radio discipline, not from arcade chaos.

### 4. Professional Evaluation

Feedback should sound like a captain or instructor, not a game over screen.

### 5. Verified Maritime Logic

No maritime rule becomes final training content until reviewed and marked approved.

## Relationship To Existing Platform

Watch Officer is the second product in `game.brkovic.ltd`.

Captain Ether remains the active radio/Sea Speak game. Watch Officer may reuse VHF/VTS learning ideas later, but it must not overwrite Captain Ether content, scoring, routes, or API endpoints.
