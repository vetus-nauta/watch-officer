# First Brief - Audio Direction / Sound Design Lead

**Role:** CHAT-AUDIO-001  
**Status:** Waiting for Game Director task assignment

## Problem To Solve

Watch Officer needs an audio direction that supports calm focus, maritime presence, readable system feedback, and psychological comfort.

Current prototype audio is not the final target.

The game must not become:

- arcade;
- cinematic action;
- alarm-heavy;
- noisy ambience;
- music-dominated;
- dependent on sound for critical information.

## Desired Feel

The player should feel:

- calm;
- focused;
- oriented;
- professionally guided;
- not startled;
- not punished by fail sounds;
- not fatigued by repeated ambience or warnings.

## Draft Audio Principles

- Background music should be light, sparse, and steady.
- One-hour loops should avoid obvious seams and repetition fatigue.
- Water should be present but not loud or hiss-heavy.
- Birds should be rare, natural, and non-loop-obvious.
- Wind and environmental layers should be subtle and optional.
- UI sounds should be short, soft, and consistent.
- Success cues should confirm calmly.
- Fail cues should guide correction without shame.
- Warning sounds should escalate by urgency without panic.
- Audio should reinforce visual/text feedback, not replace it.
