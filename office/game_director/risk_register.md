# Risk Register

## RISK-001 - Unverified Maritime Education Content

**Status:** Open  
**Impact:** High  
**Risk:** Incorrect IALA/MAMS or COLREGS logic could teach unsafe behaviour.  
**Mitigation:** Mark uncertain rules as requiring verification and route them through QA before use as final content.

## RISK-002 - Scope Expansion Before MVP

**Status:** Open  
**Impact:** High  
**Risk:** Open world, multiplayer, full hydrodynamics, advanced radar, or complex night mode could overwhelm the MVP.  
**Mitigation:** Keep MVP limited to day mode, calm weather, open water plus simple channel, motor yacht, basic marks, one traffic vessel, and simple VTS interaction.

## RISK-003 - Portal Routing Ambiguity

**Status:** Open  
**Impact:** Medium  
**Risk:** The existing game and `Nav Desk` card cannot be safely connected without knowing the portal repository and current routing.  
**Mitigation:** Confirm repository, current game route, and game-card schema before writing portal code.
