# Handoff Templates

## Department Handoff

```markdown
# Department Handoff

**From:**  
**To:**  
**Date:**  
**Status:** Draft / Ready  

## Context

What the receiving department needs to know.

## Approved Decisions

Links to relevant decision log entries.

## Inputs

Documents, assets, assumptions, and constraints.

## Required Output

Expected report, document, prototype, or validation result.

## Boundaries

What must not be changed by the receiving department.

## Open Questions

Questions requiring Game Director decision.
```
