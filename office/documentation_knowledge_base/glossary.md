# Glossary

## AIS

Automatic Identification System. In this product, AIS shows cooperative reported target information.

## COLREGS

International Regulations for Preventing Collisions at Sea. Product use must be verified before final educational presentation.

## IALA / MAMS

Maritime buoyage and marking systems. Product use must be precise and verified.

## Scenario

The main playable unit: a compact maritime situation with objectives, decision points, fail states, and evaluation criteria.

## VHF / VTS

Radio communication and vessel traffic service interactions used for concise scenario prompts.
