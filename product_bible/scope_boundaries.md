# Scope Boundaries

## Approved MVP Direction

The MVP should stay strict and small.

Included:

- Day mode only.
- Calm weather.
- Open water plus simple channel.
- Motor yacht only.
- Cardinal marks.
- Lateral marks.
- Isolated danger mark.
- Safe water mark.
- Shallow zones.
- Crossing, head-on, and overtaking COLREGS situations.
- One traffic vessel per scenario, maximum two later in MVP.
- One simple VTS popup interaction.
- Heading-up view.
- Player vessel in lower third.
- AIS target vector display.
- Simple captain report after a scenario.

## Excluded From MVP

- Open world.
- Full hydrodynamics.
- Sailing physics.
- Radar.
- Complex night mode.
- Complex TSS.
- Full VTS system.
- DSC distress.
- Engine failures.
- Realistic water simulation.
- Multiplayer.
- MMO features.
- Large map editor.
- Combat.
- Economic systems.
- Character systems.
- Complex 3D.

## Approval Rule

Anything outside the approved MVP direction requires a Game Director decision before implementation.
