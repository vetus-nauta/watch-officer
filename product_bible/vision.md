# Product Vision

## Working Name

Watch Officer

Alternative names to keep available:

- Maritime Decision Simulator
- Calm Maritime Decision Trainer
- Navigation Decision Quest
- Bridge Watch Trainer

## Product Summary

Watch Officer is a short-session 2D top-down maritime decision simulator.

The player operates a vessel through realistic but simplified maritime situations involving buoyage systems, IALA/MAMS marks, COLREGS traffic encounters, VTS/VHF communication, day and later night navigation, AIS-assisted awareness, and basic environmental effects.

The product is not an arcade game, open world, or full marine physics simulator. It is a calm scenario-based trainer for maritime situational awareness and decision-making.

## Desired Feel

- Calm
- Serious
- Professional
- Maritime
- Atmospheric
- Clear
- Mobile-first
- Not childish
- Not arcade
- Not overloaded
