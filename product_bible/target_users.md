# Target Users

## Primary Users

- Recreational sailors and motor-yacht operators.
- Maritime students who need short focused drills.
- People preparing for watchkeeping, buoyage, COLREGS, or VHF practice.

## Secondary Users

- Instructors who need compact demonstration scenarios.
- Maritime enthusiasts who prefer practical rule-based challenges over arcade action.

## Session Fit

Target session duration: 5-7 minutes maximum.

The product should support short, repeatable, high-quality scenarios rather than long open-ended play.
