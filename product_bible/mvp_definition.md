# MVP Definition

## MVP Goal

Prove that a compact heading-up 2D maritime scenario can teach safe decision-making through visual navigation, traffic interpretation, and concise feedback.

## MVP World

- Day mode.
- Calm weather.
- Open water plus simple channel.

## MVP Navigation

- Cardinal marks.
- Lateral marks.
- Isolated danger mark.
- Safe water mark.
- Shallow zones.

## MVP Vessel

- Motor yacht only.
- Simple acceleration and speed levels.
- Heading control.
- Turn-rate abstraction.
- Slowdown and stop abstraction.
- No full hydrodynamic simulation.

## MVP Traffic

- One traffic vessel per scenario.
- Maximum two only if the first interactions are stable.
- Crossing, head-on, and overtaking situations.

## MVP Communication

- One simple VTS popup interaction.

## MVP Evaluation

- Grounding.
- Collision.
- Warning.
- Checkpoint restart.
- Simple captain report.
