# Traffic System

Status: Draft

## Principle

Traffic must appear logically and support the scenario, not randomly for entertainment.

## MVP Encounters

- Head-on.
- Crossing.
- Overtaking.

## Probability Notes

- Open sea: low probability, 5-15%.
- Harbor approach: medium probability, 20-40%.
- Narrow channel: controlled medium probability, 25-45%.
- TSS later: high probability, 60-90%.

## Rule

One well-placed tanker in a traffic separation scheme is more valuable than many random targets.
